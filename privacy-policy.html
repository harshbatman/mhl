Privacy Policy

Mahto Home Loans

Last updated: January 2026

Mahto Home Loans respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Residential address
  • Date of birth
  • PAN card details
  • Aadhaar details (only if required for loan processing)
  • Employment and income details

b) Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • Salary slips or income proofs
  • Credit-related information (CIBIL or other credit bureau data)

c) Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process home loan applications
  • Verify identity and eligibility
  • Contact you regarding loan offers and services
  • Share details with banks or financial institutions (only for loan processing)
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share your information only with:

  • Partner banks and NBFCs for loan processing
  • Credit bureaus for eligibility checks
  • Government or regulatory authorities if legally required
  • Trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business

All third parties are required to keep your information confidential.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Loss or misuse
  • Alteration or disclosure

However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Improve site functionality
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
